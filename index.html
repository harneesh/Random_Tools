Privacy Policy for Random Tools

Random Tools is a simple utility application that provides tools such as coin flip, dice roller, random number generator, and yes/no decision maker.

Information Collection

Random Tools does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The app works entirely offline and does not send any data to external servers.

Use of Permissions

Random Tools does not require sensitive permissions and does not access personal information such as location, contacts, camera, or storage.

Children's Privacy

Random Tools is safe for general audiences, including children under the age of 13. The application does not collect any personal data from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

Random Tools does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or tracking technologies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If the app introduces new features in the future that affect user data, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact the developer.
